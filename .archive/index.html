premise

A Game for the Dead

Some call this place hell. They’re wrong. Hell implies punishment, judgment, a sentence handed down by something greater than you. But this world offers none of those things. No one is chosen. No one is spared. No one is owed a future. Everyone arrives the same way: dead, lost, and given one impossible gift—a second chance. What happens next is yours alone. You can survive. You can rise. You can become something greater than you ever were. Or you can disappear. The world does not care.

Welcome to The Fool. The card of beginnings. Of innocence. Of reckless faith. The step taken before the path reveals itself. The fall before you learn whether you can fly.
