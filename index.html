A Game for the Dead

Some call this place hell. They're wrong. Hell implies punishment, judgment, a sentence handed down by something greater than you. But this world offers none of those things. No one is chosen. No one is spared. No one is owed a future. Everyone arrives the same way: dead, lost, and given one gift—a second chance. What happens next is yours alone.

Welcome to The Fool. The card of beginnings. Of innocence. Of reckless faith.
