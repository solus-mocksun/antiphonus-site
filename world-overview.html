The Game

what it is

If you die with an extremely strong will to live, you do not simply end. You get pulled into a constructed world built around a 22-stage game. No one fully understands who made it or why. Everyone who arrives was mid-stride when their life cut short.

Survive all 22 stages and you go home. That is the rule. What nobody says out loud is what you come back as.

entry

Entry is not chosen. Players arrive in M00 — the starting stage — without explanation. The world does not introduce itself.

death inside the game

Death inside the game is permanent. There is no respawn. The stages are designed — by luck, by violence, or by the choices you make under pressure — to remove people.

between stages

After clearing a stage, players enter the cities — rest zones where the game pauses. Time there is real time. Some people stay for years, taking escort work, protection jobs, the kind of labor that does not get named.
