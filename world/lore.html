Lore

the returned

One person has cleared all 22 stages. He went home. Years had passed. Nobody was waiting. Everything was different. He came back — not out of defeat, but because everything he had was here: reputation, resources, the only people who knew who he was.

His presence in the story is the pivot — the moment Anti and Nyx realize that winning does not answer the question. It only changes what the question is.

who built it

Unknown. The world is too deliberate to be random. The 22-stage structure, the tarot naming, the currency system — these are designed. The question of who built this world, and why, is not answered directly. It may never be.

the leaderboard

A public ranking exists across all four cities showing how many stages each known player has cleared. It creates its own culture — respect, rivalry, myth. Nyx is ranked in the top five. Most people on the board have been there for years and have stopped trying to move.
