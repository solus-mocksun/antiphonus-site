Time & Currency

time as currency

The currency of this world is time — literally. Time remaining, time borrowed, time owed. The exact mechanics are deliberately opaque in the early stages. Anti works it out faster than most.

how it works

Every person who enters carries an implicit ledger. Time can be spent, earned, stolen, traded. The cities run on it. The stages consume it. Some players spend years in the cities building reserves before attempting the next stage.

note

The full mechanics are still being developed and will be revealed through the story.
